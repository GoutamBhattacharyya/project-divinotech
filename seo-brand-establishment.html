SEO|SMM

Digital marketing provides an organization with the opportunity of promoting and advertising their products/services digitally. Uploading articles, posts, photos and videos through social media platforms and blogs is part of the marketing strategy. It enables to send bulk emails and publicize website link, which reflects on the search engines, gradually bringing the website link on top of the search results.

  • Brand Establishment
  • Powerful marketing channel
  • Better Market Understanding
  • Better Customer Service
  • Risk Management

Brand Establishment

SEO/SMM helps the brand to grow. It enables the business to expand and if rightly executed, allows the brand to reach out to potential customers. The benefits of SEO/SMM are - attracting quality traffic, helping in PR activities, enabling the brand to stay ahead in the competition, generating organic traffic, and improving search engine ranking.

Powerful marketing channel

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) and Social Media Marketing (SMM) can forward an extensive number of traffic to the site. Increased traffic generates leads. This is the most effective method for streamlining the online presence of any business. In addition, it creates results that are strong and effective.

Better Market Understanding

SEO & SMM is essential for building up a brand. It also helps in understanding ongoing market trends. By browsing through various posts and listings an organization can analyze the current status and decide upon their future marketing propositions.

Better Customer Service

As social media platforms are interactive themselves, they act as a bridge for visitors to get in touch directly with the organization and speak their minds.

Risk Management

Social media platforms aid in nursing up a brand hit by a crisis. It helps them to communicate directly with their customers with needed explanations and preventive measures minimizing the risk. Competitive analysis helps to zero in on the crisis. It also can be used to spread the microsites build up to divert the traffic and manage the crisis safeguarding the website from further hits. This also helps in keeping up with the reputation.

Increase brand awareness

Social Media Marketing (SMM) helps spread awareness about the company and its services/products. Such exercise encourages the targeted audience to relate and get associated with the brand. The process leads to an increase in website traffic, lead generation and brand recognition.

  • Brand recognition

    Brand recognition is all about making the targeted audience familiar with the brand, along with its identity. Such an approach includes advertisement of the products or services. This encourages the customers to relate to the brand and remember its name, thus increasing its brand value.

  • Increases organic traffic

    SEO/SMM prompts exponential increment in organic traffic by publishing quality content on a regular basis, spreading referral links and execution of SEO activities.

    • Local SEO

      Local SEO techniques help in promoting the brand’s website or application, for a region-specific inquiry generated through Search Engines like Google, Bing etc. Such procedures improve the website/app ranking for local audiences.

    • Post Permalinks

      Permalinks are permanent URLs of the pages and blog entries that redirect the viewers to specific sections or categories of a web page. Permalink not only incorporates the domain name, but also the part that redirects to a specific section. This enables the client to get the URL and share it with ease. Sharing permalinks through blogs, articles and posts makes the URL stronger and helps improve its search engine ranking.

  • Grow a long term audience

    Regular updates of quality content and posts on social media platforms and blogs keeps the audience refreshed. It also helps to build a follower-subscriber base by spreading brand awareness.

    • Blog posting

      Through blog posts, an organization can publish contents regarding its products/services, events or in-house activities. Photographs, illustrations, video files or recordings, along with proper descriptive articles, inform the viewers about the different aspects of the business. Proper categorization of blogs, along with publication of links and trackbacks, aids in the optimization of search engine ranking.

    • Article posting

      Publishing contents related to public interests or brand activities helps in generating engagement among the viewers. The articles which are updated with back-links, redirect the viewers to the website or the app and aids in increasing the user base.

Better user experience

Displaying contents accordingly help visitors comprehend their requirements and get associated with the website. SEO and SMM activities provide analytical inputs of visitors’ behavior, while they were browsing the site. These analytical inputs also provide a clear perspective about the calculations to be done for structuring the website or the user interface. In order to provide better user experience, one should consider the following factors: creating a clear navigational path on the website, user focused product names and descriptions, page speed, easy navigation, bounce rate, descriptive content, and link structure.

  • Improves click through rate

    Click through rate (CTR) helps to derive better PPC (Pay per Click) results. CTR can be improved by understanding the user needs and re-arranging the products/services highlights accordingly. Such planning helps in achieving better click-through rate, while running promotions or ads. It also improves the possibility of having better conversions.

    • Content optimization

      Optimizing the content using the right words and phrases and relevant links, encourage the viewers to gain access to the website and improve CTR.

  • Improves speed of the site

    Maintaining good page-loading speed helps the users to navigate through the website with ease. It enhances the user experience and increases the stay-time. By conditioning and calculating the sizes of images, illustrations, data flow etc. and uploading them strategically, optimizes the page-loading time.

    • Google webmaster implementation

      Google Webmaster enhances the visibility of the website. It is an SEO tool provided by Google to enhance the performance of the website. It provides valuable inputs like Page Ranking, Keyword Rankings, Number of page views, Links Status etc. Google Webmaster, also known as Website Health Checker, always notifies the owner of the site about the crawler errors, malware/virus issues as well as the indexing activities running on the site.

    • Image optimization

      High-resolution images reduced in size help in loading the web pages faster.

  • Page interests

    It is the time spent by visitors on different pages of the site. Page interests justify the content quality and engagement with visitors. The analytical data received on page interests helps in to optimize page contents and page links.

  • Page views

    With the help of page analytical tools, the organization can see the traffic or view count on different pages of the website. It also helps in to understand the visitor’s interests and the organizations can plan to change or modify the page contents to increase the visibility of the pages. The bounce rate should be also considered while understanding the page views.

  • Demographic Reach

    A website builds the online presence of an organization. But SEO/SMM helps in to increase demographic reach as well. The demographic reach analysis helps in to understand the views happening from different geo-locations and the market demand.

  • Time on site

    This parameter tells the overall time a visitor spent on the site. This data helps to plan optimization of site loading time and page performance.
