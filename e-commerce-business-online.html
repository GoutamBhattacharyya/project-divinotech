E-Commerce

Over the last decade, E-commerce sales have grown on an average of 19% per year. Its end-to-end solutions like delivery to doorstep, cash-on-delivery, easy return and exchange policies have made it the favourite of web savvy consumers. As advertisements and promotions are less expensive on this platform, strategic marketing and brand positioning has become easier for the local businessmen.

Business Online

A businessman, who does any or all of their business using the internet, is running an online business. Conducting an online business can include buying and selling online, and providing an online service. An E-commerce platform gives the user a chance to introduce their business on the internet, thus helping the business to grow.

Improves Productivity

If the design architecture is correctly implemented, an E-commerce platform can solely manage both the sellers and buyers. Such management processes improve productivity. Administrators can also monitor their stock, orders and buyers/sellers without much of a stretch.

Increase Sales

Reaching out to the desired customers with the listed products/services, an e-commerce platform enables the organization to run promotions and offers. The provision for sending e-mails, newsletters and custom notifications can also boost up the engagement.

Customer Service

From product/service inquiry to delivery confirmation and claims management, the Customer Service Department plays a key role to ensure customer satisfaction. Pre and post-sales services boost the trust of the brand. The feedbacks received from the customers, also help to strategize improvements of the service/product.

Analysis

The administrator can analyze the customer traffic on the website. The organization can also track the sales and purchases, which helps to discern the market demand. Managing inventory, forecasting and risk management can be also performed effectively.

Securities

Data security should be implemented to avoid unauthorized access to the system. It averts accidental modification, destruction of data and also prevents from gaining administrative privileges. It safeguards the system by preventing any loss of data.

Brand identity

E-commerce enables organizations to do business transactions online. Not only does it help to develop a unique brand identity, it assists the company to expand the business as well. Easy navigation through the Products and Services page, and other features like doorstep delivery, cashless transactions etc helps in the recognition of the brand.

  • Design system

    The design system of an E-commerce platform is mostly buyer oriented. The integration of everything from the Logo to the Footer is done to ensure easy accessibility and uncomplicated buying experience of the buyers.

    • Logo and colors

      The logo and color give the first impression of the brand to the visitors. These are the first integral factors for creating brand recognition.

    • Architecture

      Proper management of products/services, structured data flow, easy navigation and uncomplecated buying process makes up of a good system architecture. This provides enhanced user experiencen thus lending credentiality to brands identity and recognition.

  • Personalized experience

    The Products or Services category on the E-commerce platform requires a specific process flow for seamless buying. Implementation of required buying process should be taken into account, considering the buying procedures of the customers. Such planning, structures the data flow and helps develop personalized buying experience.

    • Exposure to a targeted audience

      A well defined and user friendly buying process can easily attract the desired customers to identify and browse through the site. This in turn, helps to generate quality leads and creates brand recognition.

    • Trust and credibility

      The look, feel and simplicity throughout the process flow enhance credibility of the brand. Considering the customer’s needs in early premises and providing an errorless process flow can boost up the trust.

Less invasive experience

  • Showcase goods or services

    • Goods/service categorization

      An e-commerce platform can be designed considering single/multiple products or services. Managing categories and subcategories helps in understanding the order flow and prioritization. This also helps the visitors to browse the required product or services easily.

    • Everything at your fingertip

Return and claims

This section deals with the return policies and the monetary claims of the customers. The department finds out if the customer grievances are legit and executes the solution accordingly, either by refunding the amount or by replacing the purchased product.

  • Online claims and returns

    This section of the E-commerce store enables the customer to raise an online claim for either return or refund. With the Claim and Return management module, respective teams can take the initiative to return or replace the product/service, disbursing extra payments or payment transfers etc.

  • Dispute management

    On multi-vendor E-commerce platforms, there might arise a dispute between the third party seller and the customer, in terms of payment, products or services. In such a case, both parties should be brought into an amicable settlement. Dispute management modules help to manage the grievances easily by forwarding the disputed item/service information to the concerned team, for settling up the issue by providing a replacement or thorough reimbursement.

  • Easy Replacement

    This section allows the respective teams to manage replacement against an order id. Such features are also integrated with stock management modules to identify the replaced items and manage procurement with respect to the initiated replacement processes.
