WEBSITE

Having a business, but not a website, results in the loss of great opportunities. The website can be used as a 24/7/365 marketing tool, to not only sell a product or service, but also to inform potential customers about what the company does. Having a website instils confidence in the patrons about the capabilities of the organization.

  • Online Presence
  • Create Oppurtunity
  • Increase Sales
  • Customer Service Online
  • Real Time Tracking
  • Security

Online Presence

97% of customers search the internet, before making a purchase. If the potential customers are online, the business should also be. It is important for outbound marketing since it enhances the brand value and what the company offers to the target consumer group.

Create Oppurtunity

In today’s economy, merely building an organisation is not enough. It takes new business building practises to not only attract customers, but also investors. A website is the perfect platform to bring in more business, advertise about the services (or products) provided and also to hire personnel.

Increase sales

A website is the first impression which the company leaves on potential customers. With 93% of business decisions starting with an online search, a user friendly website with all the products (or services) showcased, will influence a greater number of people, resulting in an increase in sales.

Customer service online

Developing a strong customer service feature is extremely important for connecting with potential patrons, as well as for retaining the old ones. It improves brand reputation and helps the company understand buyer needs.

Real time tracking

Using analytical tools, a company can follow the interests and behavioral statistics of its customers, which in turn helps the organization to formulate new marketing strategies. The statistics also helps the organization to decide if any changes are required in their products/services.

Security

An unprotected website is a security risk for both users and administrators, since it allows malware to spread and escalate. The security features of the website prevent unauthorized access and alerts the admin in case of an attempted security breach.
